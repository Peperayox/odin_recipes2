Omelettes the best off eggs

Omelettes offer a tasty way of combining eggs, cheese , spices and vegtables in a solid mix that is easy to eat. I eat them for breakfast most mornings and it's taste and health benefits should warrant you to eat them too

It's ideal for quick meals, for lightweight yet fufilling meals and por those who wish a balanced infusion of vitamins and minerals while getting all their daily protein needs

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs ( orange yolk ideally)
  • 40 grams of mozarella cheese
  • 7 cherry tomatoes
  • 40 grams of sliced ham
  • rucula
  • 1 garlic head
  1. break eggs into an adequatte sized bowl and mix with a fork until getting a homogenous color mixturer
  2. Chop cheese,ham,tomatoes,garlic and rucula into bite sized portions and pile up the bites in neet piles
  3. Spray pan with oil, set stoove to meidum heat and wait 2 minutes
  4. drop egg mixture in and wait 30 seconds
  5. neetly distribute bite size pieces of all the ingredients over the omelette
  6. put lid to pan and wait for 3 minutes
  7. when semicooked, flip one half over, and put lid, wait 2 more minutes
  8. you got an omelette!!!
